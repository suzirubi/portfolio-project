suzirubi is a nickname a former boss at nike gave me many years ago. Suzi Rubino is my real name and I was born and raised in Oregon. Finished college down in San Francisco and started working at Sunset Magazine. When my first child was born I moved back to Oregon to have a little space and money. A more formal resume is up at linked in. I found my passion in my early forties, climbing, by joining a 100-year-old climbing club here in Oregon: the Mazamas. After taking almost all of their classes (haven't done advanced snow and ice yet) I've become a hike leader and assisted with many climbing classes.
